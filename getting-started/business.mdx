<Info>
  **Prerequisite** To get started with Push.ai, you'll need to connect with your
  company's internal data systems. This responsibility usually lies with a data
  team. If you'd like help with navigating setup, you can [reach out to
  us](mailto:support@push.ai) or [book
  time](https://calendly.com/d/g3s-mwy-jpb/push-ai-intro) with someone from our
  team.
</Info>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account using your company email">
    Connect with one of our supported data sources, using either a semantic
    layer or data warehouse integration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your user details and connect your Slack user if applicable">
    Give context about you and your role. If your organization uses Slack,
    connect your Slack user to receive personalized updates in your DM.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Subscribe to Business Objects">
    Subscribe to the parts of the business you care about. Receive a personalize
    feed of updates where you already work.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

Here's how to solve some common problems when working with Push.ai:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I haven't received my Subscription">
    First, check your Subscription settings in
    [**Settings**](https://app.push.ai/settings). Otherwise, you can contact
    your administrator.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I can't find the metrics I'm looking for">
    Verify with your data team or administrator that they've defined the metrics
    you're looking for.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I'm getting an unknown error">
    Please [reach out to us](mailto:support@push.ai) and we'll be happy to help
    you get setup.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

Need additional help? Feel free to [contact us](mailto:support@push.ai) or reach out to your admin.
