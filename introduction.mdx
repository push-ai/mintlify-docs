## What is Push.ai?

Push.ai is an <Tooltip tip="Enteprise Intelligence is a proactive, automated approach to operating a business, including the ability to identify the highest value opportunities and actions at any given point in time.">enteprise intelligence</Tooltip>
 tool that helps every employee stay up-to-date with with the information that's most important.

Push.ai works by connecting with a datasource, defining a set of metrics specific to your business, and then performs automated data analysis to proactively deliver updates that increase organizational knowledge and drive action.

Users can receive AI-generated analysis and updates through  Reports and Subscriptions, directly in communication channels like Slack, email, and Microsoft Teams.
