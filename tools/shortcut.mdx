<Accordion title="Create story link">
Story links (called story relationships in the ui) allow you create semantic relationships between two stories. the parameters read like an active voice grammatical sentence: subject -&gt; verb -&gt; object. the subject story acts on the object story; the object story is the direct object of the sentence. the subject story "blocks", "duplicates", or "relates to" the object story. examples: - "story 5 blocks story 6” -- story 6 is now "blocked" until story 5 is moved to a done workflow state. - "story 2 duplicates story 1” -- story 2 represents the same body of work as story 1 (and should probably be archived). - "story 7 relates to story 3”
</Accordion>
